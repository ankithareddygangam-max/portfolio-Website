ABOUT

Hackathon 2025 is a one-day technical event where students collaborate to design and develop innovative solutions for real-world problems using technology. This event encourages creativity, teamwork, and problem-solving skills in a competitive yet learning-focused environment. Participants will gain hands-on experience, interact with mentors, and showcase their ideas to industry experts.
